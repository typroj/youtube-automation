[BODY]
Sharada Peeth — Maa Saraswati ji ka original temple. 5000 scholars, duniya ki sabse badi library, Adi Shankaracharya ne yahaan Sarvajna Peetham pe baithke debate jeeta tha. Aaj? PoK mein destroyed pada hai. Indians ko jaane tak nahi dete.
Odantapuri — India ki second oldest university, 12,000 students the. Bakhtiyar Khilji aaya, Buddhist monks ko Brahmins samjha, sabko kaat diya. University ko military base bana diya. Aaj? Bihar Sharif ka naam suna hai? Wahi hai — par kisi ko pata nahi.
Buddhist Kingdom of Khotan — Central Asia mein ek poora Buddhist kingdom tha. Sanskrit bolte the, Indian culture follow karte the. Turkic Islamic forces ne 1006 AD mein khatam kar diya. Ek poori civilization — erased. Aaj woh China ka Xinjiang hai.
Pushpagiri University, Odisha — 3rd century BCE, Ashoka ne khud banwayi thi. Xuanzang ne visit kiya, likha. Par humne ise itna ignore kiya ki iska excavation 1937 mein shuru hua — aur aaj bhi complete nahi hai.
Aur sabse badi galti? Hum kabhi unite nahi hue. Jab Ghazni attack kar raha tha — Rajput kings apas mein lad rahe the. Jab Khilji universities jala raha tha — kisi ne defend nahi kiya. Jab Kashmir ka Sikandar Butshikan har ek Hindu-Buddhist temple tod raha tha — koi alliance nahi bana.
