[TITLE]
712 AD — Woh Saal Jisne Sab Badal Diya

[HOOK]
Ek 17 saal ka Arab general. 6000 soldiers. Aur ek Hindu king jise uske apne logon ne dhoka diya.

[BODY]
712 AD. Muhammad Bin Qasim — sirf 17 saal ka — Arab se ek army leke Sindh mein ghusa. Uska target tha Raja Dahir — Sindh ka Hindu Brahmin king.
Par yeh invasion itni asaan kyun hui? Kyunki Raja Dahir ke apne logon ne — kuch Buddhist governors aur dissatisfied chiefs ne — Qasim ka saath diya. Internal division ne ek kingdom khatam kar diya.
Debal port pe pehla attack hua. Temples todhe gaye. Brahmins ko mara gaya. Raja Dahir khud jung mein ladta hua maara gaya. Uski betiyan — Suryadevi aur Parimal Devi — Caliph ke paas Baghdad bhej di gayin.
Chachnama — ek 13th century text jo is invasion ko document karta hai — mein likha hai ki Sindh ke temples loot kar unka gold Arab bheja gaya.
Aur yahan se ek naya pattern shuru hua — invasion, temple destruction, conversion, aur local divisions ka exploitation. Yeh pattern agle 1000 saal repeat hota raha.
Par sabse badi galti? Raja Dahir akela lada. Na Rajputs aaye, na Pratiharas, na Kashmir ke rulers. Sab apne apne kingdoms mein baithe rahe. Ek ek karke sabki baari aayi.
