[TITLE]
Xuanzang Ne Kya Dekha Afghanistan Mein

[HOOK]
7th century mein ek Chinese monk 16 saal India travel karta raha. Usne jo likha — woh aaj ka evidence hai.

[BODY]
Xuanzang — ek Buddhist monk jo 629 AD mein China se nikla aur India aaya Buddhist texts lene. Usne poore route ka detailed record likha.
Afghanistan ke baare mein usne kya likha? Balkh mein — 100 se zyada Buddhist monasteries the aur 3000 monks rehte the. Bamiyan mein — giant Buddha statues gold se decorated the. Kapisha mein — Hindu temples the jahaan ash-smeared ascetics aur skull-wearing Shaiva sadhus the.
Yeh ek eyewitness account hai — kisi Indian ya Pakistani historian ka nahi, ek Chinese traveler ka. Unbiased, documented, aur internationally accepted.
Faxian, Song Yun, Wou-Kong — aur bhi Chinese travelers ne 399 AD se 751 AD ke beech Afghanistan visit kiya aur sab ne Hindu-Buddhist civilization describe ki.


[CLOSER]
6 Chinese travelers, 350 saal ka time span, sab ka ek hi observation — yeh land Hindu aur Buddhist tha. Kal — Kafiristan ki kahani, jahan naam badal ke identity mitayi gayi.
