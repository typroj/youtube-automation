Mohenjo-Daro. Sindh, Pakistan. 2500 BC — jab Egyptian pyramids ban rahe the, tab Indus Valley mein ek city thi jisme planned roads the, covered drains the, public baths the, aur grain storage system tha jo aaj ke engineers ko bhi shock karta hai.
Har ghar mein private well tha. Har ghar mein bathroom tha. Great Bath — ek massive public pool jisme waterproofing technology use hui thi jo aaj bhi researchers samajhne ki koshish kar rahe hain.
Population? 40,000 se zyada — ek time pe duniya ke sabse bade cities mein se ek.
Aur Pashupati Seal? Ek seal mili jismein ek figure hai — padmasana mein baitha hua, animals se ghira hua. Scholars kehte hain yeh Proto-Shiva hai. Matlab — Shiv ji ki worship Indus Valley se aa rahi hai — 4500 saal pehle.
