Yeh History Tumhe Kisi Ne Nahi Bataayi

[HOOK]
Kya tumhe pata hai ki Kabul mein kabhi Ganesh ji ki murti thi?

[BODY]
Aaj se 1400 saal pehle, Afghanistan mein Hindu temples the. Cambodia mein duniya ka sabse bada Hindu mandir hai — Angkor Wat. Indonesia mein aaj bhi Bali ke log Hindu hain.
Persia mein Zoroastrian the, Central Asia mein Buddhist monasteries the, Pakistan mein Takshashila — duniya ki pehli university — thi.
Yeh sab — archaeological evidence se proven hai. Coins, sculptures, inscriptions, Chinese travelers ke accounts — sab documented hai.
Par sawaal yeh hai — yeh sab kahan gaya? Yeh civilizations kaise gayab ho gayi?

[CLOSER]
Agle 90 din, main tumhe woh history dikhaunga jo tumhari textbooks mein nahi hai. Sirf facts. Sirf evidence. Follow karo — yeh series tumhari soch badal degi.
