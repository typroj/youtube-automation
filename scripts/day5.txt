[TITLE]
Jab Greek Aur Buddhist Art Mile

[HOOK]
Buddha ki pehli human sculpture — India mein nahi, Afghanistan-Pakistan border pe bani.

[BODY]
Gandhara — yeh region aaj ke Peshawar, Taxila, aur eastern Afghanistan mein tha. Yahaan kuch aisa hua jo art history mein unique hai — Greek sculpture style aur Buddhist philosophy ka fusion.
Alexander the Great ke baad Greek settlers yahaan bas gaye the. Unki art tradition aur local Buddhist faith ne milke ek naya art form create kiya — Gandhara Art.
Buddha statues mein Greek-style draped robes, curly hair, realistic facial features — yeh sab Gandhara ki den hai. Pehle Buddhism mein Buddha ko human form mein depict nahi karte the — sirf symbols use hote the.
Aaj yeh sculptures British Museum London mein hain, Met Museum New York mein hain. Par woh land jahaan yeh bane — wahaan ab kuch nahi bacha.


[CLOSER]
India ki art tradition ne duniya ko influence kiya — par hum khud bhool gaye. Kal — Chinese traveler Xuanzang ne Afghanistan mein kya dekha.
