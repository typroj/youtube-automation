Thailand Ke PM Ko 'Rama' Kyun Kehte Hain? Kyunki Unka Desh Humara Tha

[HOOK]
Thailand ke king ka official title hai — "Rama." Capital ka purana naam hai — "Ayutthaya." Ab batao — yeh coincidence hai ya history?

[BODY]
Thailand. Buddhist desh. Par poori foundation Hindu hai.
"Ayutthaya" — unki purani capital — yeh naam aaya hai "Ayodhya" se. Direct Sanskrit. 1351 se 1767 tak yeh capital tha.
Aaj ka capital Bangkok? Full naam jaante ho? "Krung Thep Maha Nakhon" — jo Guinness Book mein duniya ka sabse lamba city name hai — poora Sanskrit mein hai. Translation? "City of Angels, Great City of Immortals, Magnificent City of Nine Gems." Pure Devnagari concepts.
Thai king ka title "Rama" hai — Bhagwan Ram ke naam pe. Current king Rama X hai. Unka official emblem Garuda hai — Bhagwan Vishnu ka vahaan.
Thai classical dance? Ramakien — jo Ramayana ka Thai version hai. Thai script? Brahmi-based. Thai calendar? Hindu-Buddhist mixed.
Aur sabse shocking — Thai greeting "Sawadee" aata hai Sanskrit word "Svasti" se — jiska matlab hai "well-being." Har roz 70 million Thai log Sanskrit word bol rahe hain — bina jaane.
