70 Crore Muslims Ke Beech Ek Hindu Island — Aaj Bhi Zinda

[HOOK]
Indonesia — 27 crore log, 87% Muslim. Usme ek island hai jahaan aaj bhi 90% log HINDU hain. Naam hai Bali.

[BODY]
Bali. Duniya ka ek akela aisa jagah jahaan Hinduism survive kiya — poore Islamic takeover ke baavjood.
Jab 15th-16th century mein Java ke Hindu Majapahit empire ko Islamic sultanates ne defeat kiya — Hindu priests, artists, warriors Bali bhag gaye. Unhone apni sanskriti wahaan zinda rakhi. 500 saal se.
Aaj Bali mein 20,000+ Hindu temples hain. Har ghar ke saamne offerings hain — phool, dhoop. Nyepi — unka Silent Day — poora island band hota hai. Airport band. Internet band. Lights band. 24 ghante silence. Duniya mein aur kahan hota hai yeh?
Galungan festival — good vs evil — 210 din ke Balinese Hindu calendar pe based. Saraswati Day celebrate karte hain — Maa Saraswati ka din — jismein books aur instruments ko puja karte hain.
Par 1952 mein Indonesian government ne officially declare kiya ki Bali ko "Islamic proselytization campaign" ka target banana hai. Bali ne resist kiya. 1958 mein joint petition diya — Sanskrit mantra quote karke — aur apna Hindu identity bacha liya.

[CLOSER]
Jab poora Java convert ho gaya — Bali ne kaha "Nahi." Aaj bhi keh raha hai. Yeh hai resistance ka asli example.
