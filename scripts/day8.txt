[BODY]
Jab Europe mein log cave paintings bana rahe the — tab Hindustan mein 10,500 students ek university mein padh rahe the. Babylon se aate the, Greece se aate the, Syria, Egypt, China se aate the.
68 subjects — surgery, astronomy, warfare, music, politics, Ayurveda, encryption, treasure hunting — 2700 saal pehle. Admission test itna mushkil tha ki 10 mein se sirf 3 pass hote the.
Chanakya ne yahaan padha. Charaka ne yahaan padha. Panini — jisne Sanskrit ka grammar likha aur aaj MIT ka Noam Chomsky kehta hai ki linguistics ki origin Panini se hai — woh Takshashila ka product hai.
Aur aaj? UNESCO World Heritage Site hai — par Pakistan mein. Ek aam Indian ke liye visit karna almost impossible.
