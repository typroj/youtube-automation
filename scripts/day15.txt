[TITLE]
Duniya Ka Sabse Bada Mandir — Humara Hai, Par Humara Nahi

[HOOK]
Duniya ka sabse bada religious monument ek HINDU temple hai. Par woh India mein nahi — Cambodia mein hai.

[BODY]
Angkor Wat. 400 acres. Bhagwan Vishnu ko dedicated. 3 lakh majdoor. 30 saal. 50 lakh ton sandstone. King Suryavarman II ne 12th century mein banwaya — naam Sanskrit hai — "Surya" + "Varman" — "Surya ka Rakshak."
Deewarein? Samudra Manthan carved hai. Ramayan carved hai. Mahabharat carved hai. Pura temple Mount Meru ka replica hai — Hindu cosmology ke hisaab se.
Cambodia ka purana naam jaante ho? "Kambuja" — Sanskrit word. Unka naya saal Hindu calendar se aata hai. Unke classical dance mein Apsara hai — Apsara! Direct Sanskrit se.
Aaj Angkor Wat Cambodia ke flag pe hai. Unki economy ka 50% tourism isi temple se aata hai. 20 lakh tourists har saal — $100 million+ revenue.
Humne banaya. Woh kama rahe hain.
