[TITLE]
1500 Saal Khade Rahe, Phir dynamite se Uda Diye Gaye

[HOOK]
March 2001 — Taliban ne dynamite se uda diya duniya ka sabse bada Buddha statue.

[BODY]
Bamiyan, Afghanistan. Yahaan do massive Buddha statues the — ek 55 metre, doosra 38 metre. Yeh 6th century mein banaye gaye the — matlab lagbhag 1500 saal pehle.
Chinese traveler Xuanzang ne 7th century mein likha tha ki yeh statues sone se decorated the aur hazaaron monks yahaan rehte the.
UNESCO ne inhe World Heritage Site declare kiya tha. Phir bhi 2001 mein Taliban leader Mullah Omar ne order diya — "Yeh idols hain, destroy karo."
Duniya ne protest kiya. Par kuch nahi hua. Dynamite se uda diye gaye.


[CLOSER]
1500 saal ki heritage, kuch seconds mein khatam. Aur yeh pehli baar nahi hua — yeh pattern hai. Kal baat karenge Kabul ke Hindu kings ki.
