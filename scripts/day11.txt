[TITLE]
Peshawar Ka Original Naam Jaante Ho? — Purushapura

[HOOK]
Peshawar. Aaj Taliban ka stronghold hai. 2000 saal pehle? Buddhism ka global headquarters tha.

[BODY]
Peshawar ka asli naam tha Purushapura — Sanskrit mein "City of Men." Yeh Gandhara Kingdom ka capital tha — ek aisa kingdom jahan Buddhism apne peak pe tha.
Kushan Emperor Kanishka ne 2nd century mein yahaan duniya ka sabse uncha Buddhist stupa banwaya tha — Kanishka Stupa — 400 feet se zyada uncha. Reference ke liye — Qutub Minar 238 feet hai. Matlab — Kanishka ka stupa Qutub Minar se almost double tha.
Chinese traveler Xuanzang ne 7th century mein likha ki Purushapura mein 1000 se zyada Buddhist monasteries the.
Gandhara Art — woh unique art form jismein Buddha ko pehli baar human form mein depict kiya gaya — yahaan bani. Greek sculptors aur Buddhist philosophy ka fusion. Duniya ke museums mein crore mein bikti hai.
Aur aaj? Peshawar mein ek bhi functioning Buddhist monastery nahi hai. Zero.

[CLOSER]
Ek city jiska original naam Sanskrit tha, jahan duniya ka sabse uncha stupa tha — aaj wahaan se woh identity poori tarah se mita di gayi hai. Kal — Hinglaj Mata, Balochistan.
