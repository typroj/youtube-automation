Maa Sati Ka Sar Yahaan Gira Tha — Aaj Visa Lagta Hai Darshan Ke Liye

[HOOK]
51 Shakti Peethon mein sabse powerful — jahaan Maa Sati ka sar gira — woh aaj Pakistan mein hai. Tumhe jaane ke liye visa chahiye.

[BODY]
Hinglaj Mata Mandir. Balochistan, Pakistan. 51 Shakti Peethon mein se sabse important — kyunki yahaan Maa Sati ka Brahmarandhra gira tha — matlab sar, consciousness ka centre.
Yeh koi bada carved temple nahi hai. Ek natural cave hai — registan ke beech, Hingol River ke kinare. Koi idol nahi — ek pathar sindoor se ranga hua — usi ko Maa Hinglaj maante hain. Thousands of years se.
Aur jaano kya interesting hai? Local Baloch Muslims isse "Nani ka Mandir" kehte hain. Woh pilgrimage ko "Nani ki Haj" kehte hain. Sufi saint Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai ne khud Hinglaj Mata ke darshan kiye the aur unpe kavita likhi thi.
Har saal 2,50,000 se zyada log Hinglaj Yatra karte hain — yeh Pakistan ki sabse badi Hindu pilgrimage hai.
Par ek baar Islamists ne plan banaya tha — Hingol River pe dam banake cave temple ko duba do. Hindu community ke protest ke baad plan ruka.
