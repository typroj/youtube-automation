Vietnam Mein Hindu Rajao Ne 1500 Saal Raaj Kiya — Tumne Suna Bhi Nahi

[HOOK]
Vietnam. Communism. Buddhism. Yahi yaad aata hai na? Par 1500 saal tak wahaan HINDU rajas ne raaj kiya. Naam tha — Champa.

[BODY]
Champa Kingdom. 2nd century se 17th century tak — yeh duniya ke sabse lambe chalne waale Hindu kingdoms mein se ek tha. Central aur Southern Vietnam mein.
My Son Sanctuary — UNESCO World Heritage Site — wahaan 70+ Hindu temples the. Shiv ko dedicated. Sanskrit inscriptions mile hain — 3rd century ke. 3rd century! Jab Europe mein Roman Empire toot raha tha — tab Vietnam mein Sanskrit mein Shiv ki puja ho rahi thi.
Champa ke kings ke naam the — Bhadravarman, Indravarman, Jaya Simhavarman. Pure Sanskrit. Unki script Indian Brahmi se aayi thi.
Phir Vietnamese kingdoms ne dheere dheere Champa ko conquer kiya. Aur 15th-17th century mein Champa khatam ho gaya. Aaj Cham log Vietnam mein minority hain — kuch Hindu, zyaadatar Muslim ho chuke hain.
Par My Son ke temples aaj bhi khade hain — broken, bombed by Americans in Vietnam War — par khade hain.
