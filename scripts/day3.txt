[TITLE]
Jab Kabul Mein Ganesh Puja Hoti Thi

[HOOK]
Afghanistan ke Kabul mein ek Hindu dynasty ne 200 saal tak raj kiya. Naam tha — Hindu Shahi.

[BODY]
850 AD se 1026 AD tak, Hindu Shahi dynasty ne Kabul Valley aur Gandhara region — yaani aaj ka eastern Afghanistan aur northern Pakistan — yahan rule kiya.
Archaeological evidence? Bahut hai. Kabul ke paas Gardez mein marble Ganesh statue mila. Ghazni mein Durga ki colossal clay sculpture mili. Coins mile jisme "Shri Samanta Deva" likha hai — Sanskrit mein.
Yeh dynasty Hinduism aur Buddhism dono ko support karti thi.
Phir kya hua? Mahmud of Ghazni ne 17 baar attack kiya. 1026 tak Hindu Shahi dynasty completely khatam ho gayi.


[CLOSER]
Ek 200 saal purani dynasty, archaeological evidence se proven — par kitne Indians ko iska pata hai? Kal — Afghanistan ka buried Buddhist city.
