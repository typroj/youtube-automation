[TITLE]
Taliban Ne Buddhas Ke Chehere Toda — Kyunki Chehra Hona Bhi Gunah Tha

[HOOK]
2007 — Pakistan ke Swat Valley mein Taliban ne Buddha statues ke faces tod diye. Reason? Islam mein face depict karna haraam hai.

[BODY]
Swat Valley — aaj Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Par ek time pe iska naam tha Uddiyana — Sanskrit mein "Garden" — aur yeh Buddhist learning ka ek major centre tha.
Xuanzang ne 7th century mein likha — Swat mein 1400 Buddhist monasteries the. 1400! Aur 18,000 monks yahaan rehte the.
Archaeology ne confirm kiya — Butkara ruins, Barikot excavations, Saidu Sharif stupa — sab evidence hai ki yeh region centuries tak Buddhist tha.
2007-2009 mein Taliban ne Swat pe kabza kiya. Unke pehle kaam mein se ek tha — Buddha statues destroy karna. Jehandad Buddha — ek 7th century ki 6-metre carving — uska face hammer se toda gaya.
Fazlullah — Taliban commander — ne radio pe announce kiya: "Yeh but-parasti hai. Islam mein yeh allowed nahi."
2000 saal ki heritage. Ek hammer. Kuch minutes.


[CLOSER]
1400 monasteries the. 18,000 monks the. Aaj — ek bhi nahi. Swat Valley Buddhism ka garden tha — aaj woh naam bhi koi nahi jaanta. Kal — 712 AD, the turning point.
