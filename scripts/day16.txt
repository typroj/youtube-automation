Indonesia Mein 240 Hindu Temples Hain — Ek Complex Mein

[HOOK]
Duniya ka sabse bada Muslim desh — Indonesia. Wahaan 240 Hindu temples ka ek complex hai. Abhi bhi khada hai.

[BODY]
Prambanan. Java, Indonesia. 9th century. 240 temples ek saath — Shiv, Vishnu, Brahma — Trimurti ko dedicated. Main Shiv temple 47 metre uncha — deewarein Ramayana se carved.
Ek "Candi" — yeh word aata hai "Chandika" se — Maa Durga ka ek roop. Matlab Indonesia mein temples ka naam hi Durga se aaya hai.
Par suno yeh — 4th century se 15th century tak Java mein Hindu kingdoms the — Mataram, Kediri, Singhasari, Majapahit. Majapahit empire itna bada tha ki aaj ke Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines ka region cover karta tha. Hindu empire.
Phir kya hua? Islam aaya. Sultanates bane. Majapahit ke Hindu leaders Bali bhag gaye. Java — jo centuries tak Hindu tha — convert ho gaya.
Par Prambanan aaj bhi khada hai. Aur Balinese Hindus aaj bhi wahaan Galungan aur Nyepi celebrate karte hain.
