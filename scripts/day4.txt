[TITLE]
Afghanistan Ka Buried Buddhist City

[HOOK]
Kabul se sirf 40 km door, zameen ke neeche ek poora Buddhist city daba hua hai.

[BODY]
Mes Aynak, Logar Province, Afghanistan. 2010 mein archaeologists ne yahaan 42 se zyada Buddhist relics discover kiye. Stupas, Buddha statues, gold aur silver coins, frescos — kuch toh 2nd century ke hain.
Do bade Buddha statues mile — ek 7 metre, doosra 9 metre lamba. Colourful paintings mili jo gold se decorated thi.
Afghan Archaeological Department ke Mohammad Nader Rasouli ne kaha — "Kuch evidence toh prehistoric era ke bhi ho sakte hain."
Par problem? Is site ke neeche copper reserves hain — billions of dollars ki. Chinese mining company ko contract diya gaya. Heritage vs Money ki ladaai chal rahi hai.

[CLOSER]
Ek civilization jo hazaron saal zameen mein dabi rahi — aur ab mining ke liye phir se destroy hone ka khatra. Kal — Gandhara Art ki kahani.
